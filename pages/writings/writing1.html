About Me

A gently evolving self‑portrait: interests, philosophies, shifting identities, influences, and the quiet threads connecting everything I make.

Welcome to Fisholophy

This page is a living document—a place where I try to decode myself, my interests, and my philosophies. Expect reflective essays, evolving notes, and experimental fragments—each one a small dive beneath the surface.

Why Fisholophy?

Fisholophy is my informal name for this collection. It's playful—but also serious. The name helps remind me that ideas swim and shift, and that philosophy can be curious and small as much as it can be grand.

"Thinking is like water—always moving, rarely still."

I'm interested in identity, technology, quiet human moments, and the odd poetic tangent. This page will change as I do.
