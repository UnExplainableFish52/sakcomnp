an attempt to know onself:

Last updated: Sep 11, 2025
I find humans quite intriguing. The more one tries to understand oneself, the more one realizes they couldn't have been more wrong.

I find it funny when people boldly claim to know someone just because they know their name, age, and have perhaps met them in person. They might be partly right, but I prefer to define "knowing someone" in a more philosophical sense. DONT CLICK

In this writing, we will try to understand ourselves better

We all are interesting in our own ways however what I am more interested in, are how unpredictable our life is, the certainty within the uncertainty, our emotions, ideas and values.

I was actually planning on writing about me, like real simple intro but then it would not be any philosophical, so I kind of twisted it into writing about us, yes you, me and all of us. It was a casual day when I noticed that usually people have hundreds to thousands of connections on social media and life but when they are really sad, happy, confused, excited, or have any other feelings, they have none to share it with. Of course, it is not the same for all of us, most of us are fine with the current system however I believe there's more we can do. Most of us are in a weird situation, we aren't actually busy but we dont have time either. When we are talking to someone or texting someone, we respond in an instictive way, with no proper thinking, researchers believe this is a common issue in these young gen peoples because that is how we all are. Do you also find the concept of actually developing a meaningful bond with like minded people?
